About Us

The Code Breakers Club is a university club dedicated to exploring the fascinating world of coding. We offer a variety of activities and events that will help you develop your coding skills and meet other coding enthusiasts.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced coder, you're welcome to join our CODEBLAZZE EVENT and become a part of our vibrant community.

Events

  • Coding Competition

    Join us for our annual coding competition where you can showcase your coding skills and win amazing prizes.

  • Workshop on Web Development

    Learn about web development and create your own website in our interactive workshop.

  • Hackathon

    Join us for our hackathon where you can work in teams to solve real-world coding challenges and win prizes.
